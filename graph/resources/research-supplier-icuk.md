# ICUK Research Dump

**Source**: `https://www.icuk.net/platforms/telecoms-reseller` (and related subpages), retrieved May 2026.

Raw research findings on ICUK Computing Services Ltd as a potential wholesale/reseller platform partner for Sun Turtle Internet.

## Company Overview

- Founded 2001 as a dial-up reseller (via CIX), evolved into own network
- UK-based, privately owned, 90% of staff in development and support (not sales)
- 800+ resellers currently using the platform
- Contact: 0345 00 99 175 / sales@icuk.net
- UK head office with 7-days-a-week phone/email support

## Platform Pricing (all ex VAT)

| Platform | Monthly | Quarterly | Annually |
|---|---|---|---|
| Leased Line Reseller | Free (requires an Ethernet order) | — | — |
| Telecoms & Connectivity Reseller | £20 | £55 | £200 |
| Reseller Web Hosting | £20 | £55 | £200 |
| Enterprise (All-in-One) | £50 | £137.50 | £500 |

No extra fees for API access. No minimum spend commitments, no deposits.

## Service Details

### Broadband (Wholesale)
- FTTP up to 1Gbps, SoGEA/SoGFast up to 330Mbps, SoADSL up to 24Mbps
- Carriers: BT Wholesale, CityFibre
- FREE installation on FTTP and SoGEA (new connections and migrations)
- Automated ordering, provisioning, and fault handling
- Unlimited usage, no traffic shaping, no fair usage policies
- Care levels: Standard, Enhanced, Critical (7-hour fix)
- Own authentication realm (username@yourcompany.co.uk)
- Real-time diagnostics: RADIUS logs, session history, SNR reset, estate mapping
- Speed limiting, session termination, debt control (instant suspension)

### Ethernet Leased Lines
- Uncontended up to 10Gbps
- Integrated with Openreach, Sky, BT Wholesale, TalkTalk, Virgin Media, COLT
- Inclusive managed routers (up to 10Gbps)
- Router management via Control Panel
- Instant online quoting (rebrandable, shareable)
- Online order placement and tracking

### VoIP
- Hosted VoIP and SIP Trunks
- Auto-provisioning 100+ devices across 7 manufacturers (Yealink, Cisco, etc.) plus softphones
- Drag-and-drop call flow designer
- IVR/caller menus, call conferencing (10 participants per room), call recording
- Number management, porting, emergency services
- Address books with LDAP sync to devices and CRM
- Call heatmaps, per-user/group/account statistics
- PAYG and bundled minutes packages
- Tariff design management, itemised billing, CDR files
- Flexible 1-month contracts
- DDoS protected, encrypted storage, multiple data centres
- Fraud prevention alerts, junk call blocking

### One Touch Switching (OTS)
- Claimed first UK wholesale provider with complete OTS solution
- Full management — no direct TOTSCo interaction
- No extra ICUK charges (just TOTSCo fees for ICUK-service customers)
- Support for customers with third-party connections
- Brandable message content and appearance
- Handles contractual obligations for transfer-away requests
- Full gainer-led support for new customer migrations

### Other Services
- SMS (outbound)
- Fax to Email / Email to Fax

## White-Label Capabilities

- Full HTML template customisation of customer Control Panel
- Bespoke CSS, own images, customisable home page
- Rebrandable: login pages, service status pages, speed tests, Ethernet quoting tool
- Invoicing platform adds logo to bills
- Own authentication realms for broadband
- Genuinely white-label — ICUK founder was frustrated with pseudo-white-label suppliers as a reseller

## Control Panel
- Reseller Control Panel (main management)
- Customer Control Panel (fully white-label, end-user facing)
- Staff Control Panel (permission-based, multi-tiered for reseller staff)

## API
- REST-based, single API across all platforms
- Interactive Swagger docs (test/live modes)
- Multi-user access with API permissions and IP restrictions
- Code examples: C# ASP.Net, PHP, Classic ASP
- No extra subscription fees

## Network
- Multi-homed, multi-POP across several UK data centres
- 100% core uptime claimed
- DDoS-protected with scrubbing and monitoring
- Own RADIUS servers and IP ranges
- Self-built network infrastructure

## Target Market / Positioning
- Resellers of all sizes: "one-man bands to multi-million pound companies"
- No growth targets, no minimums, no deposits
- "The customers you win are your customers, not ours"
- Billing relationship belongs to the reseller
- Resellers can take all customers if they leave
