# Manifesto

Published at `/manifesto` on the website. The founding statement of why Sun Internet exists and what it believes.

## Mission

A **company limited by guarantee** providing affordable, ethical internet access in the UK. Not for profit — for sustainability, community, and impact.

## Goals

1. **Build a sustainable ISP** — resilient infrastructure, long-term viable
2. **Give back** — share revenue with Internet Archive, The Wildlife Trusts, and Shelter (specific percentage TBD once revenue is real)
3. **Fair compensation** — pay a living salary only when financially viable

## Promises

- **Transparency** — revenue, salaries, donations published
- **Community-first** — service quality over shareholder returns
- **Environmental responsibility** — minimise footprint, support green initiatives
- **Independence** — no venture capital, no external investors, accountable only to mission and community

## Open-source roadmap

Commits to open-sourcing in order:
1. Financials
2. Operations and bookkeeping — evolving toward an open-source bookkeeping system as an alternative to the current SaaS tool
3. Network infrastructure — with the ambition of eventually replacing proprietary wholesale systems with open-source alternatives

This reflects the long-term intention: QuickFile today, own system tomorrow. ICUK today, own wholesale stack tomorrow. The roadmap is a direction, not a timeline.
