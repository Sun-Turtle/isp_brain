# CLG Incorporation Process

Question: why are Green ISPs memorandum of association a different format from ours/governnment draft?

Government draft: https://assets.publishing.service.gov.uk/media/5a7c4587e5274a2041cf2e21/pro_forma_of_ca_2006_memorandum_for_a_company_without_a_share_capital.pdf
GreenISP version: [GreenISP Memorandum of Association](graph/resources/greenisp-incorporation.md)

A: Before 2006, they had different format, then Companies law changed. Now a much shorter format, and 'Objects' (goals) are no longer required - you can leave undefined when incorporating.

Q: why does the Articles of Association and Memorandum of Association have shorter/fewer sections now? (no 'Seal', no 'Objects')
A: GOV.UK/companies house shortened their requiremetns in 2006.
Before (e.g. GreenISP): Memorandum of Association + Articles of Association required

A: only Articles of Association (Memorandum of Association is just a short table; often auto-generated on incorporation)
