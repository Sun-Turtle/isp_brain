# Market Landscape

## Lead UK references

These are useful north stars for "small team, high automation, technical honesty" — exactly the lane an AI-first reseller would want to study (from the outside).

### Andrews & Arnold (A&A)

| Dimension | Notes |
|---|---|
| Positioning | Famous for no-nonsense, tech-literate service; IPv6, static IPs, geeks welcome |
| Why they matter | Proof that a niche ISP can win on trust + capability without looking like BT adverts |
| Pattern to steal | Automation, clear docs, community/forum energy, low traditional marketing |

### Aquiss

| Dimension | Notes |
|---|---|
| Positioning | Smaller UK ISP, lean footprint, broadband-first focus |
| Why they matter | Proof that non-huge brands can stay viable with focused ops |
| Pattern to steal | Efficient ops, loyal niche, avoid spray-and-pray TV ads |

### Green ISP

Based in Hebden Bridge, West Yorkshire. Founded 2003 by Paul Palmer.

| Dimension | Notes |
|---|---|
| Structure | Not-for-profit, Company Limited by Guarantee. No shareholders — the structure itself is the mission. |
| Team | ~2 people (Paul + apprentice). Rest is outsourced infrastructure. |
| Network | BT Openreach 21CN (FTTC/FTTP/SoGEA) via BT Wholesale + LLU via Opal/Tiscali. Also leased lines. Also web hosting. |
| Products | ADSL, FTTC/SoGEA, FTTP up to 1Gbps, leased ethernet lines. |
| Ethos | First UK ISP to take environmentalism seriously. Solar-powered office, 100% renewable energy (Good Energy), carbon positive (tree per customer via TreeSponsibility). |
| Recognition | Ethical Consumer Best Buy 4 times. |
| Why they matter | Proof that a ~2-person ISP can survive and win on ethos alone. No automation gimmicks, no AI — just genuine conviction and excellent personal support. The opposite pole from our AI-first thesis, but equally valid. |

Key takeaway: Green ISP validates that tiny headcount ISPs are viable. They prove the "not-for-profit + personal service" lane works. Our AI-first lane is a different bet — automation instead of personal touch — but the size proof is real.

Not claiming their exact headcount or stack — treat as a cultural and strategic reference.
