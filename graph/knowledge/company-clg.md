# Company Limited by Guarantee

A CLG has no shares and no shareholders. Members guarantee a nominal amount (typically £1) if the company is wound up. Profits are reinvested — no dividends.

Green ISP used this structure. Founded 2003, ~2 staff, still running on Openreach.

## Key characteristics

- **No share capital** — can't raise equity. Startup capital via director's loan.
- **Members, not shareholders** — members control the company, typically the same people as directors. No requirement to make employees or customers members.
- **Not-for-profit, not a charity** — directors draw a salary. Surplus gets reinvested. No Charity Commission unless you also register as a charity.
- **Flexible post-incorporation** — members, address, and name can all be changed easily after incorporation.

## Two things you file to incorporate

Incorporation = one process. Two documents:

[Incorporation](company-clg-incorporation.md) — the overall process (IN01 form, what you need, timeline, post-incorporation steps)
