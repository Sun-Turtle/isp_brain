# Privacy Policy

Published at `privacy.html` on the website. Required under UK GDPR before collecting any personal data. Drafted with a human tone following the Green ISP model while covering the lawful-basis framework from TalkTalk's policy.

## Key stances

- **No cookies, no tracking**: The website sets nothing. Theme preference is localStorage only.
- **Payment data**: We never see or store card/bank details — passed directly to processor. May change if billing setup evolves.
- **No marketing, no profiling**: We do not sell data, build advertising profiles, or send marketing.
- **Lawful bases**: Contract (service delivery), legal obligation (Ofcom, HMRC records), legitimate interest (account comms, aggregated network data).

## Data collected (customers only)

Name, service/billing address, email, phone, payment method (processor-only), IP assignment, line details.

## Third-party sharing

Wholesale platform provider (data processor), Openreach (line provisioning), payment processor, regulators/authorities when legally required.

## Retention

Duration of customer relationship + 6 years (UK limitation period + HMRC requirements).

## Rights

Standard UK GDPR rights plus contact address (privacy@suninternet.co.uk) and ICO escalation route.
